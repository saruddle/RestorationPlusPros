Restoration Plus Pros is your one stop shop for all things construction!

With over 20 years in the business of remodeling, repairing and building new structures the people who make up Restoration Plus Pros are experts in many things. Why? Because we have done many things. We would love to put our experience to work for you and either help make your dreams come true or your nightmare disappear. We can handle any job, from foundations and flooring to the roof (and everything in between). Commercial or residential. Home owners and investors have learned to depend on us. You can, too.

What We Can Do For You

Remodeling

We take what is worn out and old and make it new again. We have remodeled dozens of projects from barns to commercial buildings and everything in between.

Repair

We can handle repairs both large and small. Structural or cosmetic. Sagging floors? Leaks in the basement or from the roof? Crumbling foundation? Old Windows? Call us.

Mold & Water Remediation

Water damage isn't just an eyesore. It can lead to much larger problems and can be hazardous to your health. We are certified mold remediation specialists and will help you sort out any and all issues related to water damage.

New Construction

Whether you are looking for a general contractor to oversee your project or a complete (start to finish) solution we can help you. Foundations. Stick Frame. Steel. Commercial or residential.
